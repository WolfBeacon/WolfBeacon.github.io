General Guidelines

These guidelines outline the general rules when using WolfBeacon's brand assets and showcasing WolfBeacon content. Consistent use of these assets helps people easily recognize references to WolfBeacon and protect company trademarks.

Do's

Do maintain the shape and proportion of each of WolfBeacon's brand assets and reproduce assets at a legible size.

Don'ts

Don’t assert rights over the WolfBeacon brand whether by trademark registration, domain name registration or anything else.

Don’t modify WolfBeacon brand assets in any way, such as by changing the design or color

Don’t use:

  • Trademarks, names, domain names, logos or other content that imitates or could be confused with WolfBeacon
  • Any icons, images or trademarks to represent WolfBeacon other than what is found on this resource center
  • Any combination of any part of the WolfBeacon brand with your name, marks or generic terms
  • WolfBeacon materials with pornography, illegal activities, or other materials that violate the WolfBeacon Terms
