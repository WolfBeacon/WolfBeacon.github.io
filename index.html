Hackathon Organization & Aggregation

WolfBeacon is the world's first ever hackathon organization and aggregation platform. Built collaboratively by university students, from all seven continents, WolfBeacon streamlines the process of searching for hackathons and serves as an all-in-one package when it comes to building your own hackathons.

How it works

Everything you need in one platform, right out of the box.

WolfBeacon platforms are multi-native, utiltizes geolocation technologies and updates in real-time displaying changes the second you make it.
